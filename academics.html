Academics

I participate in many academic competitions from all subjects, including literary contests, science fairs, and math contests. In 2020, I made the honour roll in the Pascal University of Waterloo math contest, helped my team win 2nd place at Math Challengers within the Vancouver region, finalized in the Vancouver district science fair, and won first place for the Senior Essay category in the Burnaby-Vancouver region for Canada's Remembrance Day Literary contest, among other awards from years previous. This year, despite the challenges of Covid-19, I was a Gold award finalist (top 40 writers) in the Senior Essay category for the Queen's Commonwealth Essay Competition and a finalist in the 2021 Math Challengers.
