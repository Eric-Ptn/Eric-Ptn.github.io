Overview

I am volunteering for a medical research group based out of the Royal Columbian Hospital in Burnaby. The group is developing better and more accessible ways to deliver intravenous anesthesia, read EEG files and train anethesiologists. I help design the syringe pump enclosure, write abstracts for conferences, and do some outreach for the group.
