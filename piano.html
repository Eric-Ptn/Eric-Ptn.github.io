Piano

Ever since Eric was five, he's been learning the piano, and he continues today. He started partaking in competitions early on, winning numerous scholarships and awards from BCCM (The British Columbian Conservatory of Music). He later started participating in the Vancouver Kiwanis festival, being nominated to the piano provincials as both an Alternate and an Observer (2nd and 3rd place). In 2019, he won 3rd place in the Steinway Junior Competition, and additionally, earned the opportunity to perform at a concert in Italy with one of their own orchestras. Though his enrollment in UTP has given him less time for extracurriculars, he continues to pursue music. Eric has obtained grade 10 in piano and music theory, and is currently completing his ARCT diploma. Eric has experience as a piano teacher, instructing piano level 5.
