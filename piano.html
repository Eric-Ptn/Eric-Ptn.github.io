Piano

Since I was five, I've been learning to play the piano. I started partaking in competitions early on, winning scholarships and awards from The British Columbian Conservatory of Music. I later started participating in the Vancouver Kiwanis festival, being nominated to the piano provincials as both an Alternate and an Observer (2nd and 3rd place). In 2019, I won 3rd place in the Steinway Junior Competition, and later earned the opportunity to perform at a concert in Italy with one of their own orchestras. Recently I've obtained my ARCT diploma in piano and music theory. I also have experience as a piano teacher, instructing piano level 5.
